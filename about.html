The Fetch Mission

The Fetch Mission is to improve and extend every Dog’s life by giving them the love, excitement and joy they give us, we believe this starts in their bowl.

The Fetch Vision

We envision a future where every bowl is filled with love and every dog is seen as a true member of the family.

Our story starts
with Charlie

Charlie was a lively and playful dog, sadly in 2015 at the age of 8 he started having terrible joint pain. It quickly progressed, limiting his ability to walk. Concerned his pup parents, Paul and Mary took him to the vet to find that he was dealing with autoimmune health issues.

The vet explained that it was his diet of dry kibble. She suggested they begin making Charlie's food and explained to them key ingredients that could improve his health. Mary quickly got to work preparing meals weekly for Charlie.

Charlie's issues quickly cleared away. He was back to running, playing and being full of life. Mary had been devoting a significant amount of time to Charlie and couldn’t keep up with all the cooking anymore. Paul and Mary decided to look into different solutions in the market, but all they could find were frozen meals, packaged in difficult to use plastic.

After months of searching for the right solution with no luck, Paul and Mary decided to create it. They met with a vet nutritionist and began working on fresh recipes of what they called puppy meatloaf, we call Puploaf. Partnering with local chefs they brought their healthy recipes to life with popping fresh flavor.

Sadly Charlie passed away in November 2021 at age 14, but his legacy lives on as Fetch continues to grow and spread health, fresh and amazing taste to dogs across the Pacific Northwest.

A fresh revolution

Fetch meat loafs are baked in a USDA-approved commercial kitchen in southeast Portland. The facility is only used for the production of human based foods which ensures your best friend’s Puploaf is not being produced in a facility that is contaminated with the types of ingredients expected with traditional kibble or wet can food for dogs.
