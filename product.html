Feeding guide

Enclosed you’ll find the Puploaf you selected, along with a Fetch cutting board (handwash only) to assist with portion control. Based on Vinny’s weight, size and activity level you should cut a 1 1/8 inch slice per meal, assuming you feed Vinny twice a day. Feed hot or cold!

We suggest a 7 day transition period. This will allow your dog’s stomach to adjust to the new diet. If you notice your dog’s poop is runny, stay on that transition stage for an extra 2 days, or until their poop is back to normal.
